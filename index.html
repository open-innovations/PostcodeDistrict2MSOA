Postcode District to MSOA

Sometimes we only have data by Postcode District and we need values by MSOA. Postcode Districts and MSOAs don't share borders and aren't heirarchical so it is not possible to uniquely map from Postcode District to MSOA; a Postcode District may contain parts or all of several MSOAs. We will attempt to produce figures for each MSOA but you should note that the MSOA values should be treated as approximate, not correct. Different methods of mapping will produce different MSOA values.
